From Diagnostics to Operational Support

The FRP tools are designed not only to support diagnostics and benchmarking, but also to strengthen operational decision-making, reform sequencing, and regional peer learning. Over time, the platform will integrate dynamic dashboards, downloadable datasets, scenario simulations, and implementation guidance aligned with the five pillars of fiscal resilience.
